Jeff Haffner (B.A. - Ashland University; M.F.A. - University of Georgia) is an adjunct instructor of theatre at Ashland University, and also the video host for the Cleveland Orchestra's Music Explorers program. Recent credits: "Holmes 3" in CATCO'S Holmes and Watson, "Tommy" in The End of the Tour (Seat of the Pants Productions), "Palmer" in Yankee Tavern (none too fragile theatre), "Richard/Garnet" in Equivocation (Rubber City Theatre), "Sir Danvers Carew" in Jekyll and Hyde (French Creek Theatre), "Nick O'Brien" and "Eddie Lawrence" in Shear Madness (Shadowland Stages, Circa '21 Dinner Playhouse, The Palace Theatre in the Dells, and twice at the Old Creamery Theatre).
